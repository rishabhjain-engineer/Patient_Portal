1. Will I experience any pain or discomfort?
You may feel slight pain during the sample pickup process but be assured our collection team is highly trained to ensure that the discomfort is minimal.

2. How long will the sample collection process take?
The sample collection usually takes a maximum time of 10-15 minutes.

3. What are the available payment options?
If you selected Cash on Sample Pickup (COS) then please pay the full amount in cash to the lab representative after sample collection. There is an option to pay online on a few packages (option if available would be visible during the booking process).

4. Are there any other charges?
Home sample collection is absolutely free of cost. However, labs may charge for delivering hard copies of your reports.
